Instructions

Movies often contain common character types: Heros, Villains, Romantic Interests, etc..

  • Hero/Protagonist/Main Character - Leads the narrative; usually looking for something or trying to solve something. e.g. James Bond, Indiana Jones, Han Solo, Harry Potter, Batman
  • Villain/Antagonist - Struggles against the hero and gets in their way - e.g. Darth Vader, Voldemort, The Joker
  • Spouse/Partner/Lover - Romantic interest/companion of the hero or villain
  • Sidekick - Helps the hero/villain in their objective - e.g. Robin (Batman), John Watson (Sherlock Holmes), Donkey (Shrek)
  • Supporting Role Characters - Other characters in supporting roles (parents, teachers, secretaries, taxi drivers)
  • Mentor - teaches and guides the hero - e.g. Yoda, Mufasa, Gandalf, Dumbledore

We want you to indentify character types based on a summary of a movie's plot.

Your task is to:

  • Familiarise yourself with the List of Character types above.
  • Use your knowledge of the movie (if you've seen it) and the provided plot summary to understand the character's roles. Feel free to search the internet if you need more information.
  • Select the character type that applies to each character in the movie. Not all movies will have characters of all types, and some movies will have multiple characters of a single type. Pick "No Applicable Type" when nothing fits.
